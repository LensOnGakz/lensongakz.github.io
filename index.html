Welcome to LensOnGakz

Welcome to LensOnGakz, where every frame has a tale to tell. I'm Trevor, a photographer with a burning desire to photograph cities after many years of experience in that field. My journey began amidst urban landscapes, where gritty texture and raw emotion interplayed with subtle shadows and light to inspire me. My varied work encompasses the whole spectrum from spontaneous street photography to premeditated studio shoots, with every work being about truth and artistic intention.

My Unique Approach to Storytelling

A genuine devotion to storytelling distinguishes most of my photography from the others. I set out to catch the quiet defiance in a mundane moment and capture a fashion statement that glows under neon city lights. Over the years, I've partnered with creatives, models, and brands in making visual content that speaks. I engage deeply and intuitively in my process, usually contrasting traditional pictorial architectural inversions with a cinematographic flair to imprint a heavy emotion.

Explore and Collaborate with LensOnGakz

LensOnGakz is more than just a portfolio; it's a visual diary, a creative playground, and a gateway for collaboration. I invite you to explore my work, connect with the visuals, and reach out when you're ready to create something bold, beautiful, and unforgettable.Where words fall short, my camera speaks.
